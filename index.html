SUBJECTS

Upgrade Your Skill,
Upgrade Your Life

The following are some of the subjects that students
will study when they enter our school. Of course our
subjects are based on the latest IT curriculum.

01   Programming

Learn programming languages with teachers who are experts in
their fields and get national to international certifications.

02   Networking

Students will explore networking fields such as Proxy, Cisco,
and Ubiquity guided by teachers who are experts in their fields.

03   Internet of Things

Learn the internet of things in depth which will be guided by a
professional teacher.

04   Computer Assembly

Learn how to assemble computers with experts and expert
teachers in their fields
